Research

Current Research

My research is focused on understanding how our immune system and metabolism are interconnected. I’m passionate about exploring how the metabolic activities of immune cells influence health and disease, and how this knowledge can lead to discovering new treatments for conditions like autoimmune disorders, and metabolic diseases. By combining advanced computational models and artificial intelligence with cutting-edge biological research, I aim to find new ways to target these pathways, helping to design more effective and personalized therapies in the future.
