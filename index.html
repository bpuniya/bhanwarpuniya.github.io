Dr. Bhanwar Lal Puniya

Dr. Bhanwar Lal Puniya is a Research Assistant Professor at the Department of Biochemistry, University of Nebraska-Lincoln. He holds a Ph.D. in Biotechnology, an M.Sc. in Microbiology, and a B.Sc. from universities in India. Specializing in biochemistry, computational biology, and systems biology, he has extensive research experience in immune system modeling, metabolic network analysis, and drug discovery. He has been recognized with several awards and actively publishes in high-impact journals while mentoring students and conducting research in biochemistry.
