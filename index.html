Dr. Bhanwar Lal Puniya is a computational biologist currently working as a research assistant professor at the Department of Biochemistry, University of Nebraska-Lincoln. He holds a Ph.D. in Biotechnology and an M.Sc. in Microbiology. Specializing in computational and systems biology and bioinformatics, he has extensive research experience in biological data, immune system modeling, metabolic network analysis, and drug discovery. He has been recognized with several awards and actively publishes in high-impact journals while mentoring other researchers and conducting research focusing on the human immune system.
